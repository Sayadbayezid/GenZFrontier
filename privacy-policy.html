8. CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)

For California consumers, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants specific rights concerning personal data. These rights include:

  • The right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal data a business has collected about them.
  • The right to request the deletion of any personal data collected by a business.
  • The right to opt-out of the sale of their personal data by a business.
  • Upon making a request, we are obligated to respond within one month. To exercise these rights, please contact us at cwb.agency@outlook.com.

9. GDPR Data Protection Rights

Residents of the European Union (EU) and European Economic Area (EEA) are afforded comprehensive data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These rights include:

  • The right to access: You can request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate information.
  • The right to erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to restrict processing: You can request the restriction of processing.
  • The right to object to processing: You can object to our processing.
  • The right to data portability: You can request the transfer of your data.

10. Children's Information (COPPA Compliance)
