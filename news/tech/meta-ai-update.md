# Meta's New AI Tools for Digital Asset Management

Artificial Intelligence is revolutionizing how technical consultants and agency founders manage digital assets across social platforms. 

With the recent updates in the Meta ecosystem, troubleshooting monetization, managing payout configurations, and handling Rights Manager tools have become significantly more streamlined.

## Why This Matters for Agencies

For technical agencies handling multiple verified accounts, ensuring account security and proper rights management is crucial. The integration of AI workflow automation not only saves time but also reduces manual technical errors.

* **Automated Troubleshooting:** Faster resolution of payout and restriction issues.
* **Enhanced Security:** Better detection of unauthorized access for Meta Verified users.
* **Streamlined Asset Management:** Easier handling of complex assets inside Meta Business Suite.

As the digital landscape evolves in 2026, utilizing these advanced technical tools is essential for maintaining robust digital presence and sustainable growth.
