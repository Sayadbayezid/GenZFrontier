# 🏛️ Bangladesh Election Commission: Official Contact Directory & Resource Guide

**GenZ Frontier Editorial Team | June 21, 2026**

The **Bangladesh Election Commission (EC)** is an independent constitutional body responsible for organizing and overseeing all national and local elections in Bangladesh. To ensure transparency and accessibility, we have compiled this official contact directory and resource guide for citizens, researchers, and media professionals.

---

## 📱 Digital Services & Citizen Portals

The EC maintains several digital platforms to facilitate voter registration, NID services, and election monitoring.

*   **NID Services Portal:** [nidw.gov.bd](https://nidw.gov.bd) — For National ID corrections, downloads, and new registrations.
*   **Smart Election Management BD App:** Available on Google Play and Apple App Store for real-time election updates and polling station information.
*   **Postal Voting Portal:** [portal.ocv.gov.bd](https://portal.ocv.gov.bd) — Facilitating voting for government officials and Bangladeshis living abroad.
*   **Web Mail for Officials:** [mail.bcc.gov.bd](https://mail.bcc.gov.bd)
