Editorial Guidelines & Review Process

1. Submission Standards

  • All claims must be backed by verifiable sources.
  • No hate speech, defamatory content, or misinformation.
  • Plagiarism results in an immediate permanent ban.
  • Images must be copyright-free or explicitly owned by you.

2. Editorial Review

  • Our team reviews submissions within 24-48 hours.
  • We fact-check all major claims independently.
  • Our editors reserve the right to modify headlines for clarity.
  • Approved articles are published with author credits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I get paid for my submission?
Currently, guest contributions are unpaid unless explicitly commissioned by our editorial board. You will, however, receive full author attribution and backlinks where appropriate.
Can I submit an article anonymously?
Yes. If you have sensitive breaking news (a "tip"), you can request anonymity. We will verify your identity privately to ensure accuracy but protect your public identity under shield laws.
How do I know if my article was accepted?
Our editorial team will email you at the address provided in the form if your article is selected for publication or if we require further edits.
